About Us

Welcome to Benji Skate Shop, the heart and soul of skateboarding in Orange, Texas. Since our inception in 2023, we have been dedicated to bringing the skateboarding community the best selection of decks, trucks, wheels, bearings, and complete boards. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting, our goal is to provide top-notch products and unparalleled customer service to help you ride in style and with confidence. Come visit us and experience the passion for skateboarding that drives everything we do.
